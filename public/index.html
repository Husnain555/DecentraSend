How It Works
Step 1
Encrypt
AES-256-GCM encryption runs in your browser. A unique key is generated per file.
Step 2
Upload
Only the encrypted ciphertext is pinned to IPFS via Lighthouse.
Step 3
Share
Get a link with CID + key in the URL fragment. Key never reaches any server.
Step 4
Decrypt
Recipient opens the link, fetches from IPFS, decrypts in their browser.
